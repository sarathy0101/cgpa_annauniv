The formula to calculate cgpa/gpa is given below:

Ci-Number of credits to the course

GPi-Points corresponding to the grade obtained for each course

n-Number of courses

NOTE:

In case of cgpa
n-stands for no of semesters and
GPi - stands for each semester gpa and
Ci - stands for Total credits for each semester
